About Us

We really are here to help.

When my grandfather passed away, a local coin collector came to the house and offered my grandmother almost nothing for his collection. I happened to be visiting, and I knew that collection was worth far more. I priced it out and got her thousands more than that first offer.

That experience never left me. People shouldn't have to worry about being taken advantage of, especially when they're grieving. I've been helping families with estates, junk removal, and real estate since high school, and I take pride in always doing honest work.

We're not here to make millions. We're here to help.
